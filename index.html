Xuefeng Zhu

Professor @ Dalian University of Technology

I am a professor at Dalian University of Technology, specializing in isogeometric analysis and industrial simulation software research. I have proposed B++ splines, THB++ splines, and conformal embedded domain isogeometric analysis, and developed ADIGA, a CAE software platform that achieves seamless integration of CAD and CAE without mesh generation or geometry reconstruction. My current research interests focus on AI-based real-time simulation technologies for digital twins and world models.
